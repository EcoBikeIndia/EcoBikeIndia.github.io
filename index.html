Our Goal

Cities like Bombay, Delhi, Bangalore have a lot of congestion and generally alot of pollution. With EcoBike we plan to reduce the congestion by revolutionizing the premium mode of transport to electronic bikes, which would be ecofriendly.

Our Plan

Bring in electric powered bikes in several convenient locations in these cities. These bikes would simply be accessed by an app on your mobile device and can be returned. Pricing will be conducted on an hourly or daily basis for now.
